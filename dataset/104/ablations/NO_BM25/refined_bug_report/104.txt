TITLE  
ResidualVQ MLPs Initialization Issue

SYMPTOMS  
• MLPs in ResidualVQ are initialized regardless of the `implicit_neural_codebook` setting.  
• No error messages are thrown, but the behavior is inconsistent with expected functionality.  
• Initialization occurs even when `implicit_neural_codebook=False`, leading to unnecessary resource usage.

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
When `implicit_neural_codebook` is set to `False`, MLPs should not be initialized, conserving resources and adhering to the intended configuration.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Set `implicit_neural_codebook` to `False` in the ResidualVQ configuration.  
2. Instantiate the ResidualVQ class.  
3. Observe the initialization of MLPs despite the configuration setting.  
4. Verify the state of MLPs to confirm they are initialized when they should not be.
