TITLE  
ResidualVQ MLP Initialization Issue Regardless of Configuration

SYMPTOMS  
• MLPs in ResidualVQ are initialized even when `implicit_neural_codebook=False`.  
• No error messages are generated; the behavior is silent but incorrect.  
• Unwanted MLP initialization leads to unnecessary resource usage and potential performance degradation.

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
When `implicit_neural_codebook=False`, MLPs should not be initialized, conserving resources and ensuring that the system behaves as intended.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Set `implicit_neural_codebook=False` in the ResidualVQ configuration.  
2. Instantiate the ResidualVQ class.  
3. Observe that MLPs are initialized despite the configuration setting.  
4. Verify that MLP initialization occurs by inspecting the object attributes.
