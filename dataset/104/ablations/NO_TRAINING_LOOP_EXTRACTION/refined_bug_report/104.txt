TITLE  
ResidualVQ MLP Initialization Occurs Unconditionally

SYMPTOMS  
• MLPs in ResidualVQ are initialized regardless of the `implicit_neural_codebook` flag setting.  
• No error messages are generated, but the behavior is inconsistent with expected functionality.  
• This leads to unnecessary resource allocation when `implicit_neural_codebook=False`.

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
When `implicit_neural_codebook=False`, MLPs should not be initialized, conserving resources and aligning with the intended configuration.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Set `implicit_neural_codebook` to `False` in the ResidualVQ configuration.  
2. Instantiate the ResidualVQ model.  
3. Observe the initialization process to confirm that MLPs are still being created despite the flag setting.
