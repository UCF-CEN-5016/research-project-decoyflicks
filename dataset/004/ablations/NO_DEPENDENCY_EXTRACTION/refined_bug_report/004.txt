TITLE  
Incorrect Implementation of Beta Sampling in MixUp + Cutmix

SYMPTOMS  
• The sampling from a beta distribution in `tfm.vision.augment.MixupAndCutmix._sample_from_beta` is incorrect, leading to erroneous mixup samples.  
• The generated mixup samples do not conform to the expected distribution, resulting in poor quality outputs.  
• The mixup samples visually appear incorrect, as shown in the attached image.  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
When the software functions correctly, the `_sample_from_beta` method should generate samples from the beta distribution accurately, producing valid mixup samples that conform to the expected statistical properties of the beta distribution.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Import the necessary libraries: `tensorflow_models`, `tensorflow`, `numpy`, and `matplotlib`.  
2. Call the `_sample_from_beta` method with parameters `alpha=0.2`, `beta=0.2`, and a shape derived from `tf.range(1000)`.  
3. Visualize the output of the `_sample_from_beta` method using a kernel density estimate plot with `sns.kdeplot`.  
4. Compare the output against samples generated using `np.random.beta(0.2, 0.2, 100000)` in a separate plot.  
5. Observe the discrepancies between the two distributions.
