TITLE  
Incorrect Implementation of Beta Sampling in MixUp + Cutmix

SYMPTOMS  
• The sampling from a beta distribution in `tfm.vision.augment.MixupAndCutmix._sample_from_beta` is incorrect.  
• This results in poor mixup samples, leading to degraded performance in augmentation.  
• The current implementation produces outputs that do not conform to the expected beta distribution behavior.  
• An example plot shows a significant discrepancy between the generated samples and expected beta distribution samples.

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The `_sample_from_beta` method should correctly sample from a beta distribution using the specified alpha and beta parameters, resulting in outputs that follow the expected statistical properties of a beta distribution. The mixup samples should reflect a proper blend of the input data, enhancing the model's performance during training.
