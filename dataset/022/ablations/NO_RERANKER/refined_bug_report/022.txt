SYMPTOMS  
• The magnitude standard deviation in RandAugment is always 1.  
• The code logic appears to be missing a `level_std*` term after the `+=` operator, leading to this issue.  
• This results in a lack of variability in the augmentations applied, which may not be the intended behavior.

The magnitude standard deviation should be calculated correctly using the `level_std*` term, allowing for a range of standard deviation values that contribute to varied augmentations.
