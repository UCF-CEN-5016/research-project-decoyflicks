TITLE  
NaNs Propagation After Rotating Queries and Keys in Transformer Model

SYMPTOMS  
• K contains a small fraction of NaN values after the call to rotary_emb.rotate_queries_and_keys(Q, K).  
• The presence of NaNs in K leads to NaN values in the loss calculation during training.  
• The model fails to converge due to the propagation of NaNs through the transformer architecture.  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The rotation of queries and keys should not introduce any NaN values, and the loss should be a valid numerical value, allowing the model to train without interruptions or convergence issues.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Initialize the transformer model and input tensors Q and K.  
2. Call the function rotary_emb.rotate_queries_and_keys(Q, K).  
3. Inspect the tensor K for NaN values after the function call.  
4. Proceed with the training loop and monitor the loss value for NaNs.  
5. Confirm that NaNs propagate through the model and affect the loss calculation.
