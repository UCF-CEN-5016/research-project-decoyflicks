TITLE  
NaNs Introduced in K After Rotating Q and K with rotary_emb

SYMPTOMS  
• K contains a small fraction of NaNs after executing rotary_emb.rotate_queries_and_keys(Q, K)  
• NaNs propagate through the transformer, resulting in a NaN loss during training  
• Unexpected behavior observed in the model output due to NaN values  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The rotation of queries and keys should not introduce any NaN values in K or Q, and the model should produce a valid loss value without any NaNs.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Initialize the transformer model and input data for Q and K.  
2. Call the method rotary_emb.rotate_queries_and_keys(Q, K).  
3. Inspect the contents of K after the method call to check for NaN values.  
4. Proceed with the forward pass of the transformer model and monitor the loss calculation for NaN values.  
5. Document any errors or unexpected outputs encountered during the process.
