TITLE  
NaNs in K after rotating Q and K with rotary_emb

SYMPTOMS  
• After executing `rotary_emb.rotate_queries_and_keys(Q, K)`, K contains NaN values.  
• NaNs propagate through the transformer, leading to a NaN loss during training.  
• No specific error messages are reported, but the presence of NaNs affects model performance and stability.  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The rotation of Q and K should not introduce any NaN values, and the transformer should function correctly without resulting in NaN losses.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Initialize the transformer model with the rotary embedding layer.  
2. Prepare input tensors Q and K.  
3. Call `rotary_emb.rotate_queries_and_keys(Q, K)`.  
4. Check the contents of K for NaN values after the rotation.  
5. Proceed with the rest of the transformer operations and monitor the loss.
