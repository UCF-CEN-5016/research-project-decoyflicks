TITLE  
Unreasonably High Accuracy Rate on Example Dataset

SYMPTOMS  
• The model achieves a 100% accuracy rate from the first epoch.  
• Training and validation losses are extremely low (e.g., loss: 0.0075, val_loss: 0.0002) across multiple epochs.  
• The accuracy remains at 100% for all epochs without any signs of overfitting or variability.  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The model should demonstrate a more realistic accuracy rate that reflects typical training behavior, with possible fluctuations in accuracy and loss values across epochs. The accuracy should not reach 100% so quickly, indicating that the model is generalizing well rather than memorizing the training data.
