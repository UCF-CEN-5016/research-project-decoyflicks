TITLE  
Validation Accuracy Exceeds Training Accuracy During Training

SYMPTOMS  
• Validation accuracy consistently higher than training accuracy throughout the training process.  
• Observed behavior occurs with and without dropout and dropout_emb configurations.  
• No error messages reported; only unexpected behavior regarding accuracy metrics.  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The training accuracy should typically be equal to or higher than validation accuracy as the model learns from the training dataset. A significant and consistent gap where validation accuracy exceeds training accuracy may indicate an issue with model training or data handling.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Initialize the ViT model using the provided parameters:  
   ```python
   from vit_pytorch.vit_for_small_dataset import ViT
   batch_size = 64
   model = ViT(
       image_size = 224,
       patch_size = 16,
       num_classes = 2,
       dim = 1024,
       depth = 6,
       heads = 16,
       mlp_dim = 2048,
       dropout = 0.1,
       emb_dropout = 0.1)
   ```
2. Train the model on the cats and dogs dataset using the example script.  
3. Monitor and plot the training and validation accuracy throughout the training process.  
4. Compare the accuracy metrics with those of a CNN model trained on the same dataset.  
5. Analyze the resulting accuracy plots to observe the relationship between training and validation accuracy.
