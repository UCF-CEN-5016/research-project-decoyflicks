TITLE  
Validation accuracy exceeds training accuracy during model training

SYMPTOMS  
• Validation accuracy consistently higher than training accuracy throughout the training process  
• Observed behavior occurs with and without dropout and dropout_emb  
• Comparison made with a CNN model shows similar inconsistencies  
• No error messages reported  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The training accuracy should generally be equal to or higher than the validation accuracy, indicating that the model is learning effectively from the training data.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Use the example script provided for training on the cats and dogs dataset.  
2. Configure the model parameters as specified:  
   - batch_size = 64  
   - image_size = 224  
   - patch_size = 16  
   - num_classes = 2  
   - dim = 1024  
   - depth = 6  
   - heads = 16  
   - mlp_dim = 2048  
   - dropout = 0.1  
   - emb_dropout = 0.1  
3. Train the model and monitor both training and validation accuracy.  
4. Compare the results with those of a CNN model for reference.  
5. Plot the training and validation accuracy over time to visualize the discrepancy.
