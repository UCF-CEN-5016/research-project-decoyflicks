TITLE  
Encoder to_logits.weight parameter not updating during training

SYMPTOMS  
• The parameter `encoder.to_logits.weight` does not update during the training process.  
• Running Distributed Data Parallel (DDP) results in inconsistencies due to the parameter not being part of the forward pass.  
• No error messages are shown, but the model's predictions remain unchanged, indicating that the weights are not being utilized correctly.  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The `encoder.to_logits.weight` parameter should be included in the forward pass of the model, allowing it to update during training and properly contribute to the model's predictions.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Clone the repository containing the encoder/decoder implementation.  
2. Load the model and configure it for training with DDP.  
3. Execute the training loop with sample data.  
4. Monitor the updates to the `encoder.to_logits.weight` parameter during training to confirm if it changes.  
5. Verify that the model's output changes appropriately as training progresses.  
6. Observe the behavior when removing the `encoder.to_logits.weight` parameter to assess its impact on training.
