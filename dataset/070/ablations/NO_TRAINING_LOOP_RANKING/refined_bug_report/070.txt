The training script should execute without warnings or errors, successfully utilizing the specified GPUs for training the GPT-3 model without encountering issues related to device mapping or deprecated features.

   `cd DeepSpeedExamples/training/DeepSpeed-Domino`
3. Prepare the necessary datasets and files as specified in the script.
4. Execute the job script using SLURM with the provided parameters:
