• The model training process does not start and fails with the above error message.

**EXPECTED BEHAVIOR**  
The nnUNet model should successfully initiate training without raising any exceptions, utilizing the specified learning rate scheduler and training parameters.

   ```plaintext
   !python ../main.py --brats --brats22_model --scheduler --learning_rate 0.0003 --epochs 10 --fold 0 --gpus 1 --task 11 --nfolds 10 --save_ckpt
   ```  
