TITLE  
NaN Loss During Training with FP8 Precision in Transformer Engine

SYMPTOMS  
• Training with FP8 precision results in NaN loss.  
• No specific error messages are provided; the loss metric simply becomes NaN.  
• Model fails to converge, leading to ineffective training outcomes.

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The training process should yield a valid loss value that decreases over iterations, indicating proper convergence of the model.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Set up a transformer architecture using the Transformer Engine library.  
2. Configure the training to utilize FP8 precision.  
3. Initiate the training process.  
4. Monitor the loss metric during training to observe the occurrence of NaN values.
