TITLE  
NaN Loss When Training in FP8 with Transformer Engine

SYMPTOMS  
• The training process results in NaN loss values.  
• No specific error messages are provided; only the loss value becomes NaN.  
• The training appears to proceed normally until the loss becomes NaN, after which it cannot recover.  

EXPECTED BEHAVIOR  
The training process should complete without encountering NaN loss values, and the loss should decrease consistently as expected during training.

REPRODUCTION STEPS  
1. Set up a training environment using the transformer architecture.  
2. Configure the training to use FP8 precision with the Transformer Engine library.  
3. Begin training the model with a standard dataset.  
4. Monitor the loss values throughout the training process to observe when it becomes NaN.
